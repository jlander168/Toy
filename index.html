Summary

Super Smash Bros. Brawl is a fighting game for the Nintendo Wii. It was developed by Masahiro Sakurai, and published by Nintendo. Smash Bros Brawl is the third installment
in the Super Smash Bros. series.

About

Super Smash Bros is a series of crossover fighting video games published by Nintendo, and primarily features characters from various Nintendo franchises. The series was created by Masahiro Sakurai, who has directed every game in the series. Brawl was the first game in the series to have a third party character including Snake from Metal Gear Solid and Sonic from Sonic the Hedgehog.
Brawl allows the player to select from thirty seven playable characters. Dr. Mario, Roy, Young Link, Mewtwo, and Pichu were cut from the previous game but they do appear as stickers/trophies.
